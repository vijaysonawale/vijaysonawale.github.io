Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

At SarkariAI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Name, age, education level, education field, percentage, category, preferred state
  • Usage Data: Job searches, applications viewed, and interaction with our platform
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with relevant government job opportunities using AI algorithms
  • Send personalized job recommendations and notifications
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Communicate important updates about jobs and our platform
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Government Job Portals: When you apply to jobs through our platform, we may redirect you to official government websites

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure password storage with encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data we have collected
  • Correction: Update or correct your profile information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails and notifications
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login session
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Personalize your experience
  • Serve relevant advertisements (if applicable)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to government job portals and other external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing any information.

8. Children's Privacy

SarkariAI is intended for users aged 15 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 15 years of age.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or through our platform. Your continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@sarkariai.in
  • Website: https://sarkariai.in

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where required by law.

12. International Users

SarkariAI primarily serves users in India. If you access our services from outside India, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in India.

By using SarkariAI, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
