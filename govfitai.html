6. Data Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Open JobFit AI app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account & All Data"
  4. Confirm deletion

This will permanently remove your profile, saved jobs, and notification preferences. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Alternatively, email us at sonawalesvijay@gmail.com with your registered email to request deletion.

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Upon deletion, all data is permanently removed within 24 hours.
