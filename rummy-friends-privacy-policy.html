Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026  |  App Version: 1.0.0  |  Developer: Vijay Sonawale

Rummy Friends Club ("we", "us", or "our") is a social skill-based card game available on Android. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve the game experience:

  • Account data: Firebase UID, display name, and email address (Google Sign-In only). Guest accounts have no email.
  • Profile data: Nickname and avatar you choose during onboarding.
  • Gameplay data: Game history, scores, and room participation stored in Firebase Realtime Database and Cloud Firestore.
  • Device token: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications such as friend requests and room invites.
  • Payment & Withdrawal Data: To process real-money withdrawals, we may collect your UPI ID or bank account details. We DO NOT collect or store credit card or deposit information, as we do not accept user deposits.
  • Ad interaction data: Google AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers to serve rewarded video ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Purchase data: If you purchase Room Credits, the payment provider processes the payment. We only receive confirmation of the purchase, not your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your in-game account and profile.
  • To facilitate multiplayer game rooms and match you with friends.
  • To process your cash withdrawal requests.
  • To send push notifications (room invites, friend requests) — you can disable these in your device settings.
  • To serve rewarded advertisements via Google AdMob so you can earn in-game items.
  • To prevent cheating, ensure fair gameplay, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Virtual Currency & Withdrawals Important

Rummy Friends Club operates a strict ZERO-DEPOSIT model. You CANNOT deposit real money to play. We use the following systems:

  • Winnings: You can win real cash prizes in designated game modes. These winnings are added to your Withdrawable Balance and can be withdrawn to your verified bank account or UPI ID.
  • Room Credits: A one-time service fee that covers server hosting costs for creating a game room. Room Credits are a hosting service fee, not a wager. Purchasing Room Credits does not guarantee any in-game outcome or cash winnings.
  • In-game Coins: Used as stakes in specific free modes. Coins have no real-money value and cannot be withdrawn, transferred, or exchanged for real money.

4. Skill-Based Gaming & Zero Deposits

Rummy Friends Club is a skill-based game. We do not operate as a traditional gambling platform because we DO NOT allow users to deposit their own money to wager. All cash prizes are funded through the platform, and entry is managed via free or earned Room Credits. Winning depends entirely on the player's skill.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted service providers:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, RTDB, Auth, FCM) — our infrastructure provider.
  • Payment Partners — for processing your withdrawal requests (e.g., UPI transfers).
  • Google AdMob — for serving rewarded video advertisements.
  • Payment Gateways (Google Play/Razorpay) — for processing Room Credit purchases.

All third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies. We do not share your data with advertisers or marketers.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us at sonawalesvijay@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days. You can also delete your guest account by simply uninstalling the app; guest accounts with no linked Google account are automatically purged after 90 days of inactivity.

6a. How to Delete Your Account Step-by-Step

To request deletion of your Rummy Friends Club account and all associated data:

📧 Submit a Deletion Request

  1. Send an email to: sonawalesvijay@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Rummy Friends Club Account
  3. Include the following details in your email:
    • Your in-game Nickname
    • Your Google email used in the app (if applicable)

🗑️ What Data Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed, we permanently delete:

  • Account profile (nickname, avatar)
  • Email address (if Google Sign-In was used)
  • Firebase User ID
  • Game history and scores
  • Friend list and room history
  • Push notification tokens

⏱️ Retention Period

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt.

7. Children's Privacy

Rummy Friends Club is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security including Firebase Authentication, Firestore Security Rules, and HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit. Firebase data is stored in Google's secure cloud infrastructure.

9. Your Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may update your nickname and avatar at any time in-app.
  • Deletion: You may request full account deletion (see Section 6a).
  • Opt-out of ads: You may opt out of personalised ads via your device's ad settings.
  • Notifications: You may disable push notifications in your device settings.
