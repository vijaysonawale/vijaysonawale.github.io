Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

1. Zero-Deposit & Withdrawal Model

Rummy Friends Club operates strictly on a zero-deposit, skill-based gaming model. Users cannot deposit, add, or wager their own money into the app.

  • Access to real-money prize pools is provided for free or via ad-supported entries (e.g., watching rewarded ads).
  • Winnings accumulated from skill-based gameplay can be withdrawn to a valid Indian bank account or UPI ID.
  • Because no user deposits are accepted, we do not require, process, or store credit card or payment gateway information for incoming funds.

2. State & Geo-Location Restrictions

Real-money prize pools are STRICTLY PROHIBITED for users located in specific restricted regions. If you reside in or are accessing the app from these locations, you may only play the game for entertainment purposes without any cash prizes.

The restricted Indian states include, but are not limited to:

  • Andhra Pradesh
  • Assam
  • Nagaland
  • Odisha
  • Sikkim
  • Telangana

Users outside of India are also restricted to "Entertainment Mode" and are not eligible for cash withdrawals.

We actively utilize IP-based and GPS-based location tracking upon app launch and game entry to enforce these restrictions. By using the app, you consent to this location verification process to comply with local laws.

3. Data Collection for Withdrawals (KYC)

If you participate in eligible real-money modes and request a withdrawal of your winnings, we are legally required to collect specific information to process the transaction and prevent fraud:

  • Payment Details: Your UPI ID, Bank Account Number, IFSC Code, and Account Holder Name.
  • Identity Verification (KYC): Government-issued ID (such as PAN Card or Aadhaar) may be requested to verify your identity and age before a withdrawal is approved.
  • This information is used exclusively for processing your requested payouts and tax compliance. It is stored securely and never sold to third parties.

4. Standard Data Collection

To provide the game services, we collect basic account data (Firebase UID, Display Name, Email via Google Sign-In) and gameplay data (match scores, win/loss history). We do not share your gameplay history publicly beyond the necessary in-game leaderboards and match logs.

5. Age Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years of age to use Rummy Friends Club. If we determine that a user is under 18, their account will be immediately terminated, and any accumulated winnings will be forfeited.

Rummy Friends Club is a skill-based card game. Google LLC is not a sponsor of, nor is it involved in any way with, the contests, prizes, or real-money payouts provided within this application.
